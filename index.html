What We Do

Three Core Services. One Embedded Partner.

High Noon embeds as your product leader and covers the full lifecycle — defining a product vision backed by market research and reverse-engineered trends, user story creation and refinement, product design from mockups through MVP, product management through initial build, go-to-market strategy and sales enablement, and metric-driven management for continuous development cycles. Flexible enough to own the full lifecycle end to end, any individual stage, or anything in between — with your existing team or with ours. This is what we do — all of it, end to end, or focused on the areas where you need the most leverage right now.

Vision

Before anyone builds anything — or before you build the next thing — we do the market research to figure out where you can actually win. Competitive analysis, consumer segmentation, user research, economic trends — all of it goes into a data-backed roadmap that tells your team exactly what to build and why. No guessing. No building in the dark.

Learn more →

Management

Fully embedded, running the product from Day 0 through Day 2 — that's concept all the way through continuous delivery. We lead the cross-functional teams, manage the budget, keep the roadmap on track, and make sure everyone's aligned and moving in the same direction.

No stalled initiatives. No misaligned teams. Products that actually ship. On time.

Learn more →

Marketing

Building a great product is only half of it. We lead the go-to-market strategy from the ground up — persona development, positioning, messaging, sales enablement, product marketing, demand generation — everything you need to actually execute and bring revenue in.

Your product deserves a story as strong as the product itself. We make sure it has one.

We Work With Companies at Every Stage of Growth

Startup

Pre-revenue – $10M

  • Limited or no dedicated product leadership
  • Seed / Series A or bootstrapped
  • Need vision, build, and GTM under one roof

Small Business

$10M – $100M

You've got real revenue and a team — but your product motion hasn't caught up. You need someone to fine-tune current initiatives, expand your core products, or discover the next opportunities before your competition does.

  • Growth plateauing despite solid revenue
  • Roadmap driven by whoever's loudest, not data
  • Ready to scale — but need the product function to match
See how we help →

Mid-Market

$100M – $1B

You've got established revenue and a real team — but the market's maturing, the roadmap has gotten reactive, and growth is getting harder. High Noon brings an outside perspective, fresh data, and clarity to sharpen the expanded product strategy — plus the execution to help you actually differentiate and move faster than the competition.

  • Growth is slowing or market is getting competitive
  • Roadmap is reactive, not vision-led
  • GTM needs sharper positioning and execution
See how we help →

Enterprise

$1B+

Large-scale organizations — Fortune 500s, government technology programs — that need an independent product expert to come in, do the market discovery, and help identify and execute on the next big product opportunity at scale, whether that's vertical integration into adjacent markets or horizontal expansion across new segments.

  • Evaluating a new product category
  • Strategic initiative stalled internally
  • Gov-tech / regulated-industry environments

How It Works

Three Phases. No Guesswork.

Every engagement follows the same structured path — research, build, launch. It's designed so we're not figuring out what comes next. We're just moving.

Research & Position

I dig into your market — competitive landscape, customers, economic trends — and build a clear vision for where you can win and what you should actually build. This is the foundation everything else is built on.

Build & Execute

Fully embedded, running the build from Day 0 to Day 2. I manage the roadmap, the team, stakeholder alignment, timelines, and scope — so nothing falls through the cracks and you're always moving forward.

Launch & Grow

We take it to market with a complete GTM strategy. We equip and collaborate with your sales and marketing teams, drive demand generation, and put the feedback loops in place to keep growing long after initial launch.

About High Noon

Senior Business Management, Embedded Product Leadership

We started High Noon because I kept seeing the same problem — companies ripe with growth opportunities, but internal leadership was maxed out or heads-down on current roadmap initiatives — and needed an outside perspective to spot what they couldn't see from inside. So we built a model where we can come in as that senior product leader, do the work, and actually drive results. I've spent over a decade managing outcomes across companies of all sizes.

My background is a bit unusual — I've got economics and marketing on one side, and business management, Saas, and DevSecOps on the other. That combination is what lets me cover the full product lifecycle as one partner, not a rotating cast of consultants you have to manage.

Industries We're Working In

Software
